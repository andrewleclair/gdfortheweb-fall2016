Past the "Private Parking" and other threatening and proprietary signs, you can walk right by the windows of this secret house. At night, you can see in to the rooms.

An enormous german shepard guards by the window.

Firewood is stacked high on the the former front porch.

You wonder how this house was, actually, when it was the only house and the land around it was a farm, and what it was like on this porch before it was just for storage.

You also wonder about their heating situation.

Now on Vernon, the houses are smaller. You head right towards the dry cleaner and Julian's up on the corner.
