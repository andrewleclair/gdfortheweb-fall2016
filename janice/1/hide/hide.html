You see many houses as you try and find a good one to hide in.

You find a big blue house to hide in

**Knock knock**... Nobody answers and you enter

Inside is really red; everything is carpet.

You sense you need to hide in a hole fast

Your heart beats faster. You spot a little crevice!

Tons of hideous monsters pass by and you hold your breath...

Golems, vampires, ghosts, evil clowns, and possessed dolls march down the hall

Stay in the hole || Run away sneakily || Clobber them to death!
